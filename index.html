This game is made by Mayur Chandpara and Designed by Udacity Nanodegree. This is a project of my Nanodegree.(Project 2: The Memory Game)

The rules:
  • Clicking two different cards is a move.
  • You have 3 seconds to see all the cards.
  • Find all the matches in the least posible moves.
  • Stars change as per your score.
  • There is a timer for extra challange to yourself.
  • Best of luck.
