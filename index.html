Welcome to The Game of Memory!!!

This game is made by Mayur Chandpara and Designed by Udacity Nanodegree. Read the rules below to understand the game rules and click the start button to test your Memory. The game can only be played by mouse/pointing device.

The rules:

  • Clicking two different cards is a move.
  • You have 3 seconds to see and memorize all the cards.
  • Find all the matches in the least posible moves(lowest 8 possible).
  • Stars change as per your score depending on your moves and not time.
  • There is a timer for extra challange to yourself.
  • Best of luck. See you on the other side.

This is a project of my Web Developer Nanodegree. (Project 2: The Memory Game). Trying to make responsive as much as possible.
